Project

Make a styled webpage with the following:

  1. A photo
  2. A short paragraph about you
  3. A list of your interests (professional, personal)
  4. Links to your social media: LinkedIn, Twitter, Facebook, Meetup profile, etc.
  5. Short, descriptive headers for each section
