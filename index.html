I'm Devon Persing. I'm an accessibility specialist.

Who

Hi! I'm a technology generalist who works primarily on the web.

I consult, teach, and train in issues around web accessibility, usability, and development. I have a formal background in information science and writing, as well as fifteen years of experience in writing front-end code.

I think it's crucial to think about the web as our primary software platform for communication, innovation, and inclusion.

Where
